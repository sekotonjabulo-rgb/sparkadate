Frequently Asked Questions

How does blind matching work?

When you match with someone, both of your photos remain hidden. You can chat freely and get to know each other. After a period determined by our algorithm (12 hours to 5 days), you can reveal your photos to each other simultaneously.

Can I reveal my photos early?

Pro users can request an early reveal at any time. However, both parties must agree for the reveal to happen. Free users must wait until the timer expires.

What happens after the reveal?

After photos are revealed, both users are asked if they want to continue the conversation. If either person chooses to leave, the match ends and you'll be paired with someone new.

What's included in Spark Pro?

Pro members get unlimited exits, the ability to request early reveals, purchase time extensions, and shorter wait times between matches. Pro costs $20/month.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the settings page. All your data, including photos and messages, will be permanently removed within 30 days.
