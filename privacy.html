Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Spark ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Spark dating application.

Information We Collect

When you create and use a Spark account, we collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, age, gender, profile photos, general location, preferences, and messages exchanged with other users. If you choose to subscribe to Spark Pro, payment information is processed securely through our payment providers and is not stored directly on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and improve Spark, create meaningful matches, facilitate conversations, process subscriptions, communicate important updates, provide support, and ensure the safety and integrity of the platform.

Photo Privacy

Photos are central to Spark’s blind matching experience. Your photos remain hidden from matches until both users agree to reveal them or the reveal timer expires. Spark does not sell, rent, or use your photos for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. While no system is completely secure, we continuously review and improve our security practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time through the app settings. You may also opt out of non-essential communications. Account deletions permanently remove your data within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, you can contact us at support@sparkadate.online.
