Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Spark ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our dating application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email, age, gender)
  • Profile information (photos, location, preferences)
  • Messages and communications with other users
  • Payment information for premium subscriptions

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Match you with compatible users
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions

Photo Privacy

Your photos are a core part of our blind dating experience. Photos remain hidden from your matches until both parties agree to reveal or the timer expires. We do not share your photos with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@spark.app
